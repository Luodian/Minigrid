Environment Rule Description

In this environment, the objective is to use the yellow key to unlock the door and reach the green goal square. The environment is a 5x5 grid with a vertical wall dividing it at a random column position (2-3), creating left and right sections. The red triangular agent starts randomly positioned on the left side, and the goal is a green square in the bottom-right corner. A yellow locked door is placed randomly in the vertical wall, and a yellow key is placed randomly on the left side. The agent must find the key, pick it up, unlock the door, pass through, and reach the goal. Success is achieved by reaching the goal.
